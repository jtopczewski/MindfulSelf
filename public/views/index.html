THE MINDFUL SELF EXPERIMENT

Welcome to The Mindful Experiment. In this study, we want to investigate the effects of mindfulness on various aspects of the self, cognition such as flexibility, emotion and behavior. The experiment has been set up using jsPsych

There are several variants of the study, please follow our instructions and click on the corresponding link to go to the experiment page.

Working Memory Training

Questionnaires, Working Memory Training

In this study, you wil complete some questionnaires before and after the working memory training over three days.

Mindful Self Pilot Study

Questionnaires, FIST, Breath Counting

In this study, you wil complete some questionnaires before and after the breath counting exercise over three days. Additionally, you will also complete one task before and after the study.

The Mindful Self Study

Questionnaires, tasks, Breath Counting

In the full Mindful Study, you will complete several questionnaires, behavioral tasks, and breath counting exercises over several days.

The Mindful Self Study - Polysubstance

Questionnaires, tasks, Breath Counting

In this Mindful Study version, you will complete several questionnaires, behavioral tasks, and breath counting exercises over several days.
